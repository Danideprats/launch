1. Consciousness Factory

Hello, I’m Dani. Originally from Spain, I spent many years in Barcelona as a financial auditor before moving to Canada. As a financial auditor, I reviewed numerous company financial statements, which allowed me to thoroughly understand their internal processes and financial health. This experience always kept me thinking about building a tool to help individuals track their own internal processes, enabling them to take action on what matters most.

This is how the Consciousness Factory appeared—a tool designed to help you understand yourself better, to have a clear vision and mission in life, and to be able to correct and re-address those actions that are not aligned with you. This is a vibrant space where your inner world truly shapes your outer reality.

The CF is here to empower you on this journey, built on three core pillars that will support your amazing transformation: Mindset, Lifestyle and Spiritism.

2. The Holistic Healing Approach: Initial Crisis

My professional journey took unexpected turns, leading me to work in the medical sector reviewing documents for people battling illnesses. However, my own health crisis changed everything.

Everything began with a severe flare-up that landed me in the hospital for an entire month. During this time, I lost 20 kg as my body struggled to cope. That same year, I was diagnosed with two autoimmune diseases and attention deficit disorder (ADD). Managing these conditions required a complex treatment regimen — including injections every two weeks, corticosteroids, and other immunosuppressants.

⚠️ Important: Throughout my healing process, I remained committed to my prescribed medication, understanding its critical role in managing my conditions.

My doctor explained that autoimmune disorders often stem from a combination of environmental triggers, genetic predispositions, and dietary influences. These factors were always present in my mind, and I came to understand that healing the soul required more than just addressing physical symptoms — it needed a holistic approach.

3. The Deepening Journey & Emotional Reckoning

Shortly after my second autoimmune disease diagnosis, I faced a series of personal difficulties that completely crashed my soul and shattered my sense of self. Immense thoughts and emotions went through my head and soul.

In response, I sought therapy, including group therapy called ACT (Acceptance and Commitment Therapy). This approach significantly helped me to navigate my healing process by understanding my emotions, thoughts, and behaviors. It was through this profound experience that the foundations of Consciousness Factory (CF) began to take shape.

I began incorporating Spiritism, into my daily routine, seeking comfort beyond the physical realm. I shifted my mindset to embrace positivity, meditation, and visualization—practices that have profoundly influenced my well-being. Alongside this, I adjusted my Lifestyle — adopting healthier habits and routines that supported my healing.

4. Embracing Wholeness & Collective Consciousness

This experience taught me a profound lesson: true healing is multifaceted. It requires balance between physical health, emotional wellbeing, spiritual growth, and meaningful relationships. Healing is about restoring harmony within yourself and with those around you.

CF is a digital platform that grew out of my personal journey as an economist and a seeker. I am not a medical doctor—just someone who wanted to structure my own process in a way that could offer support and inspiration to others. This platform is meant to be a part of your growth journey, not a magic pill. By sharing the lessons I’ve learned, I hope to help you embrace a holistic approach to personal growth and create a powerful You that shines brightly.
